BROOK LOBELIA
Lobelia kalmii Linnaeus
Brook lobelia is abundant locally in the Canadian Rockies. The sturdiest specimens seen were growing beside a brook that found its way through a bed of disintegrated calcareous tufa. It is a dainty and pretty plant, though not to be compared in showiness with its relatives, the cardinalflower and the large blue lobelia.
